Overview-Foundation Core Skills

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

Motivation- My motivation was always based on the external reward and once I achieved it i used to become very complacent, I have to find internal motivation so that I won’t be self-satisfied once I have achieved my goal.

Self-regulation- I should think before I speak, I am very respectable to people but after going through this, it dawns on me that sometime I do say things without thinking.

Social Skills- I enjoying being alone, I don’t like to meet many people I rather spend some time alone than meeting new people. I was never liked this I used to be an extrovert person who used to meet people and make new friends, I didn’t know how this transformation happened but would like to change this aspect of my life.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

Neuroplasticity- I really enjoyed this session and was surprised by this. I knew that if you practice a lot you can become good at it but never came across any scientific study which can prove this.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

The most challenging was finding out the identity and value exploration. It took me a lot of time to think about what my values are, I have never taught so deeply onto this aspect so it was bit of a struggle for me.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

Core learning has really helped me to finish the foundation course, it does teach us about our mindset and how should we go about it to practice any new skills. I would say Core learning was the most important part in our learning journey of web development and now i understood why Dev Academy focused on core learning.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer

No it didn’t felt like a waste of time at all. As I said earlier this have really helped me to understand about myself and how to finish the foundation without taking too much of stress. I have learned a lot of things which I wasn’t aware of such as neuroplasticity, some part of emotional intelligence and finding my values and identity.
